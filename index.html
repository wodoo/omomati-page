Frequently Asked Questions

I found a bug—what should I send?

Please email us a brief description of the issue, steps to reproduce the problem, and if possible, include a screenshot or screen recording. This helps us identify and fix the issue faster.

How do I reset my account?

Open the Omomati app → Settings → Account → Reset Account

How can I request a new feature?

Email us with the subject line "Feature Request" and include a detailed explanation of your use case and how the feature would help you track your pregnancy or baby's development.

Is my pregnancy and baby data secure?

Yes, all your personal data is encrypted and stored securely. We follow industry-standard security practices to protect your sensitive information.

Can I track multiple pregnancies or babies?

Absolutely! Omomati supports tracking multiple pregnancies and babies. You can easily switch between them using the tabs in the main interface.

How do I export my data?

Currently, data export is not available, but it's on our roadmap. Email us if you need your data for any specific reason.
